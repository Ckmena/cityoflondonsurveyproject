London's Tech Sector Survey

Thank you for taking the time to participate in this survey!

The City of London and the Data Visualization program* at Fanshawe College have partnered on a project designed to provide a 'snapshot' of the London digital creative community, through a selection of relevant interactive visualizations and dashboards. The target is a Web application with a back end that could be used to continue to gather new and changing data in future. The information we are collecting in this phase will help shape the project as it develops.

The City plans to collect and visualize this information about digital creative companies as they want to use the information as part of their planning activities, from selecting good locations for possible incubator spaces, to gather some insights into the digital creative industry moving out of the first COVID lockdown, to identifying actions to help support and grow the sector. Importantly, the information gathered will allow development of an application that can more fully represent the strength and breadth of the sector in London. The City wants this application to be a step towards better representation of London everywhere as a vital and growing tech hub.


**** For questions related to COVID, for the purposes of this survey we have defined 3 time periods: ***pre, during, post lockdown lengths***
**** The information collected in this study will be visualized at the company level for available demographic information (number of employees etc.) All other information pertaining to trends, employeee skill sets etc. will be presented in a summarized fashion, with no specific company or individual information.
